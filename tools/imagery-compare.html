Satellite imagery fidelity check — Esri World Imagery vs Mapbox Satellite

Esri World Imagery (no key required)

Mapbox Satellite (needs token)

Tiles render at native 1:1 — no upscaling, so what you see is the true source resolution. Each panel is 512×512 px (2×2 tiles). Your pinpoint view needs ~1.9 m/px; z17 is 1.2 m/px at the equator and finer toward the poles.
